Life at Phyang

HIAL is located in Phyang, a beautiful village located at a distance of approx. 19 kms from the Leh airport. HIAL shares a collaborative relationship with the village that results in mutually beneficial outcomes, working with the Amalays (mothers) of Phyang for the Himalayan Farm Stays project. HIAL also organizes village walks, workshops, and activities like farmers market pop-ups featuring local products and food items to draw more tourists to the village.
Being at a convenient distance from the Leh market, one can always visit the city for shopping, dining out, or visiting major tourist spots in Leh like the Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace, Tsemo Monastery, etc. HIAL is also very accessible from many other famous tourist attractions like the Spituk Monastery, Hall of Fame, Magnetic Hill, Pathar Sahib Gurudwara, Sangam (Confluence) of Indus and Zanskar Rivers, Nimo River Rafting, etc.
