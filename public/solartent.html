Solar Tent

Overview:

Sheshyon is currently in the process of improving the design of the first version of the solar tent prototype constructed in 2021-22. Based on building performance tests conducted in the past year, we are developing designs with better thermal performance and improved design. Our goal for this project is to create a product that is lightweight, rapidly deployable and provides good thermal performance using PSH methods. We are expecting to create a prototype by August 2024.
