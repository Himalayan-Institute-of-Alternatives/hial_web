Sheshyon Innovations

We prioritize using sun and earth (and waste) to build our buildings but also work with conventional materials when needed. We are pioneers in Passive Solar Heated Building technology. To take this technology to the masses, government and Army we have started the Shesyon Innovations construction company. It promotes regenerative construction technologies to develop Ladakh as a model community for the Hindu Kush Himalayas and ultimately the mountain regions of the world.
