School of Responsible Tourism (SoRT)

The School of Responsible Tourism is pioneering the promotion of homestays in Phyang and nearby villages to encourage and create livelihood options for our Amalays (Mothers). Our Himalayan Farmstays expand across Ladakh villages, focusing on eco-friendly operations, along with collaborations with partners like UNDP and Technical University of Munich to empower local communities and explore sustainable tourism potential while having the support of eco-conscious advocates like Milind Soman and Ankita Konwar.
